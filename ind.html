Our Vision

Medicine for All

Summer Academy The Summer Academy is a rigorous medical intensive that lasts for 2 weeks. This program focuses on uplifting Latinx and Black commmunities in the medical field with workshops, simulations, and lectures for topics that students want to pursue in the future. Students who participate are encouraged to have already been part of the Winter Academy, but it is not necessary for success in this program.

Winter Academy The Winter Academy is an intensive, introductory course that lasts for 5 days. Students able to participate are those from the Latinx and Black communities who are interested in pursuing a career in medicine in the future. The course will include lectures, simulations, and projects for all who participate
