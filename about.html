Inpire Youth Medicine started when a high school student, Savir Singh, had a vision of equality across the field of medicine. After brainstorming on ways he could help underrepresented communities as just a student, he decided to create a free program dedicated to educating underrepresented students about medicine. Inspire Youth Medicine is a product of his endevaours in the summer of 2022 and already has already taken lofty steps to achieve their goal.
